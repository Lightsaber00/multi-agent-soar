Core modules

This view is designed to help a SOC analyst move through incident handling in a guided way.

Playbooks

Structured response paths for common incident categories and triage actions.

Alert Correlation

Combines related signals into a single incident context before actioning.

Ticketing

Creates and updates incidents with status, ownership, and notes.
